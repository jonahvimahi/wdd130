Our Trips

Big Mallard

Take on one of the most legendary rapids in the region. The Big Mallard is a thrilling ride that will leave you breathless and wanting more. Perfect for those with some rafting experience.

Staircase Rapids

A series of cascading drops that make for a unique and exciting experience. Great for intermediate rafters looking for a challenge without going full extreme.

Half Day Adventure

Perfect for families and beginners. This guided half-day float gives you a taste of the river with calm stretches and mild rapids, all with stunning scenery along the way.
