The Rafting Company

We believe adventure is for everyone. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned paddler, our guides will make sure your experience is safe, exciting, and unforgettable.

History

Founded in 1987 on the banks of the Snake River, The Rafting Company started as a one-raft operation run by two brothers with a shared passion for the outdoors. Over the decades we've grown into one of the most trusted outfitters in the region.

We have guided thousands of guests through breathtaking whitewater in the American West. Our mission has always been simple: make every trip safe, memorable, and worth telling stories about for years to come.
